Camping Adventures

Camp Shands

Address: 234 Shands Trail, Green Cove Springs, FL 32043

Camp Shands is a beautiful campsite offering hiking, fishing, and outdoor skills activities. Scouts should bring tent, sleeping bag, rain gear, sturdy shoes, and personal items. Recommended: compass, flashlight, cooking gear, and water bottle.

Echoketee Scout Reservation

Address: 540 Scout Rd, Perry, FL 32348

Echoketee Scout Reservation provides multiple adventure activities including archery, canoeing, and high adventure challenges. Scouts should bring tent, sleeping bag, rain gear, hiking boots, clothing for weather, toiletries, and personal gear. Optional: merit badge supplies, camera, extra socks.

Camping Adventures

Camp Shands

Address: 1400 N. Main St, St. Augustine, FL 32084

Camp Shands is our first major campsite with full amenities including dining hall, fire pits, and hiking trails. Perfect for beginner scouts and skill-building activities.

Gear to Pack: Tent, sleeping bag, backpack, water bottle, flashlight, first aid kit, rain gear, appropriate clothing for the season, and personal toiletries.

Camp Echoketee

Address: 1234 Echo Lake Rd, Gainesville, FL 32601

Camp Echoketee offers wilderness experience with canoeing, nature hikes, and survival challenges. Great for more advanced scouts seeking adventure.

Gear to Pack: Same as Camp Shands, plus canoe gear if participating in water activities, insect repellent, sunscreen, and compass/map for navigation exercises.

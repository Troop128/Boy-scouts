Camping Gear Checklist

  • Scout uniform (field shirt, pants, belt)
  • Socks and underwear (extra pairs)
  • Rain gear / poncho
  • Sleeping bag & ground pad
  • Tent (if not using troop tents)
  • Flashlight / headlamp with extra batteries
  • Water bottle / hydration system
  • Personal toiletries (toothbrush, soap, towel)
  • Hiking boots or sturdy shoes
  • Hat and sunscreen
  • Bug spray / insect repellent
  • Scout handbook & merit badge books
  • Notebook & pen/pencil
  • Mess kit / plate / cup / utensils

Meetings
